History and Growth

Regent Hill International schools are multicultural english medium private international schools registered with the Botswana Ministry of Education and Skills Development.The tradition of academic excellence encourages students to have independent thoughts interest and self disciplined study,through a rich global experience,which blends the school curriculum with PSLE in primary. In the secondary schools cambridge is exclusively offered.

Regent Hill International Schools beleives in an equal opportunity policy wich ensures an envirinment in which all members have the rights to opportunity regardless of gender,race and disability.The equal policy opportunity is structured tu augment both individual and team performance,which leads to a powerful development.

Regent Hill school opened its doors in January 2009with its first campus in Gaborone,Block 9,with an enrollment of 56 primary school level students.As its testamentto its ethos of academicand cultural execellence,the enrollment number have rapidly grown, expanding in 2014 to Metsimotlhabe campus,in 2015 to our Tlokweng campus,in 2017 to our Molepolole campus, and in 2023 to our Mochudi campus

We are confident that the growth rate will continue to increasenot only due to the demand for high quality private education but also due to the quality of our education and quality
